My Projects

Modeling Mathematics Performance

Used supervised machine learning models to predict mathematics performance of Ghanaian junior high school students, using a dataset of 364 observations. Implemented in Python with scikit-learn under the supervision of Dr. Bright Emmanuel Owusu at AIMS Ghana. The project addressed educational outcomes and informed teaching strategies.

AI-Powered Poultry Monitoring System

We're designing an AI-based system using YOLOv8 and IoT sensors to detect early signs of Newcastle Disease in poultry farms. Developed for the InnovAIMS Bootcamp, where my team (DPB) won first place and 20,000 cedis for prototyping. The system aims to reduce mortality rates among smallholder farmers in Ghana.
